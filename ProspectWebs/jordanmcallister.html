Analysis

Overview

6-foot-4, 250-pound run defender with massive lower half, good height and a wide wingspan. McAllister has a compact and durable lower body with plus burst down the second level. His production and film from 2022 are impressive but not quite as gaudy as his statistical production in 2023. His tape is littered with dominant pass-rushing burst and explosive finishing, but his effort as a run defender and ability to handle double-teams will need to improve for him to become a three-down edge defender.

Strengths

  • Expertly timed first step to win the rush edge and dip it to the outside.
  • Rides bull rush speed and heavy hands to knock back tackle into the pocket.
  • Plays with consistent burst to reach the edge and chase the quarterback.
  • Possesses good foot quickness to help him avoid blockers and chase the play.

Weaknesses

  • Slow getting his hands into contact and down the field.Takes a while to get his hands into his set and can be beat by quick hands.
  • Can struggle to sustain blocks when he starts late.
  • Could use better anticipation and awareness on pass sets.Limited foot quickness that will lead to issues at times.
  • Plays with an upright stance that reduces his ability to recover when hands get in trouble.
