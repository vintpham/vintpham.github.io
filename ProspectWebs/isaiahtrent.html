Analysis

Overview

Talented but inconsistent cornerback prospect who has flashed the potential to play on Sundays.The talent is there to cover man-to-man and he plays with good anticipation and recognition when playing zone coverage. He'll find himself in the right place more often than not, but his technique can get sloppy from time to time. He's got solid size and length that should allow him to compete against bigger receivers. There are concerns about whether he's disciplined enough to handle some of the finer points of pro coverage techniques, so it will require coaching and a strong work ethic on his part. There could be an opportunity to coach up his game once he gets into the pros, which might help raise his floor slightly higher than average.

Strengths

  • Fluid hips make for smooth transitions through breaks.
  • Has a fluid buildup speed that allows him to run and catch quickly.

Weaknesses

  • Lacks ideal foot quickness out of his plant and turn.
  • Struggles to consistently release out of press coverage.
  • Needs to improve ability to mirror releases and stay connected downfield.
  • A little too loose with positioning inside the route.
