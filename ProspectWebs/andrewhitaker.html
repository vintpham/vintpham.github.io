Analysis

Overview

Whitaker has size limitations but plays bigger than you might think given how explosive he is. When watching him play safety over linebacker I could see why some wonder if either position would be best suited long term for this physical defender who possesses terrific range once he gets moving forward as well as ball skills that can help cover tight ends in man coverage situations.

Strengths

  • Size doesn"t limit athleticism.
  • Has quickness and instinctiveness to close while making hits near the sideline.
  • Whipped around blockers en route to getting backside pursuit tackle vs. Utah

Weaknesses

  • Sniffing out underneath zone runs will take experience and recognition skill development.
  • Tries too hard to make every pass breakup look like a splashy highlight reel grab rather than just playing football soundly.
  • Struggles working through traffic and keeping feet active for open field recovery speed.
